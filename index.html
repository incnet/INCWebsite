About

Infinite Network of Creatives

  • 2011-2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Infinite Network of creatives was founded in a dormroom in a Tanzanian University by a student persuing a major in Computer science whose ambition was to primarily bring together creative people and provide a sustainable working environment for them to florish.

  • March 2014

    An Agency is Born

    An Idea became reality, the company started taking on seriouse projects from small businesses and a few of big company projects, the delivery was overwelmingly well recieved, what started as small idea had a bright future.

  • December 2016

    Transition to Full Service

    The focus became sustainability and growth, more projects were being handled under a well organised team management system, the company started taking on big projects from big companies in the likes of Puma Energy and SDS.

  • July 2019

    Phase Two Expansion

    Looking back to what inspired it all creativity remained the major driving force of the company, with it came the creative's team expansion. infinite Network of Creatives major goal has always been to protect, conserve and nurture the creative heart of African Descent.
