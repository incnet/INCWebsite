We are a youth owned and run production house with a sole purpose of enabling young intelligent upcoming artists reach their full potential. We plan on creating an opportunity for you to empower yourself or local artists by facilitating the production processes provided by the INC studios production house which will ensure activation of the full house production teams and talents who will be ready to pioneer new artists and help them exist with good sustainability and relevance in the large scale Content creation industry for instance in the music industry, writing, poetry, painting and movie industry.

INC Studios currently has a Music Production Studio with 4 Music producers, an animation studio with 4 animators, a post production house for editing videos and sound engineering with 3 editors and 2 sound engineers, and 6 accessible Directors who all have done short film and have directed several music videos each, not forgetting the steady growing Management, P.R, Marketing, sales and administration side which’s purpose is to approach corporates  and make sure the artists stays afloat. For more details you may visit INC Studios parent company " Infinite Network of creatives" page here "inc.co.tz"
